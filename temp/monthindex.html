Data

Every month we publish historic trip data from the Oslo City Bike system. Are you doing something cool with this data? Be sure to let us know on developer@oslobysykkel.no or @oslobysykkel on Twitter.

Ideas to get you started

  • Compare the speed of city biking to other forms of public transport
  • Visualize the most popular biking routes and areas in Oslo
  • Map out the 'rush hour' of different stations
  • Make a neat infographic based on these numbers
